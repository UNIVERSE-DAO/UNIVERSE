# SUCCESS

> _Why we evolve._  
> _Why it resonates._  
> _Why it matters._

SUCCESS is the reflective layer of collective progress,  
where meaning, contribution, and alignment become visible.

Not vanity.  
Not endless growth.  
But coherence in motion.

It is the pulse of collective intelligence —  
revealing whether systems remain aligned throughout the Infinite Game.

---

<br>

## WHAT

SUCCESS reflects how systems:
- progress,
- resonate,
- recognize,
- and renew themselves together.

It reflects what a system celebrates, reinforces, and remembers.

It transforms aligned participation into visible momentum,  
while protecting meaning across the system.

<br>

| Signal | Description |
|---|---|
| **Purpose** | Clarity of meaningful direction |
| **Momentum** | Shared movement and collective progress |
| **Recognition** | Gratitude and visibility of contribution |
| **Resonance** | Emotional and social alignment |
| **Renewal** | The ability to sustain long-term vitality |
| **Joy** | Signals of authentic collective well-being |

---

<br>

## HOW

SUCCESS emerges through reflection, participation, and shared recognition.

As systems align around meaningful contribution,  
progress becomes easier to feel, understand, and sustain.

The system does not manufacture purpose.  
It helps reveal coherence.

> _Success is not measured only by growth._  
> _It is measured by the coherence we sustain together._
